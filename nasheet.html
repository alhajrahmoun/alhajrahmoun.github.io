Nasheet

Overview

Go beyond counting steps. Nasheet turns your walks into cultural adventures through authentic storytelling. Follow Nasheet's journey from his hometown to destinations around the world, where your daily steps unlock rich cultural discoveries and meaningful experiences.

Key Features

Smart Step Tracking

Track your daily steps, distance, and activity while following Nasheet's journey. Your movement unlocks new destinations and cultural discoveries as you progress together.

Two-Part Discovery Experience

Arrival Stories: Reach landmarks to discover what Nasheet sees and experiences when first arriving at each location.

Deep Exploration: Add extra steps to unlock rich cultural content, traditions, and hidden stories about each place.

Journey Around the World

Starting from Nasheet's hometown, your steps will take you both on a global adventure. Unlock new cities and landmarks as you maintain your daily activity goals.

Authentic Cultural Content

Learn about architecture, traditions, and local stories through Nasheet's personal discoveries. Each location offers rich cultural insights that make your fitness journey meaningful.

Why Nasheet?

Combine your fitness goals with cultural exploration. Unlike standard step trackers, Nasheet makes every walk an opportunity to discover something new about our world. It's education and exercise combined into one meaningful experience.
